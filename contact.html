Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the air quality data updated?

Our air quality data is updated in real-time from our data provider. When you search for a city, you'll see the most current measurements available.

Which cities are covered?

We provide air quality information for major cities worldwide. Search for any city using our search bar to check availability.

Is Breeze free to use?

Yes! Breeze is completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to air quality information.

How accurate is your data?

We source our data from Open-Meteo, which aggregates measurements from official monitoring stations and follows international air quality standards.

Can I suggest a new feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Send your suggestions to hello@breeze.app.

Report an Issue

If you've encountered a bug or technical issue, please email us at hello@breeze.app with:

  • A description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Your browser and device information
  • Screenshots if applicable

We'll investigate and get back to you as soon as possible.
