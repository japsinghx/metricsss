About Breeze

Our Mission

Breeze is dedicated to providing real-time, accurate air quality information to people around the world. We believe everyone deserves to breathe clean air and make informed decisions about their outdoor activities.

What We Do

We aggregate air quality data from reliable sources and present it in a clean, easy-to-understand interface. Our platform monitors Air Quality Index (AQI) levels and provides actionable health recommendations based on current conditions.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Data: Up-to-date AQI measurements from major cities worldwide
  • Comprehensive Metrics: Track PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, Ozone, and CO levels
  • Health Recommendations: Personalized advice based on air quality conditions
  • Global Coverage: Monitor air quality in cities across the globe
  • Clean Design: Simple, modern interface inspired by iOS and Google design principles

Why Air Quality Matters

Air pollution is one of the world's leading health risks. Poor air quality can cause respiratory issues, cardiovascular problems, and reduced quality of life. By staying informed about air quality levels, you can:

  • Plan outdoor activities during safer times
  • Protect vulnerable family members (children, elderly, those with respiratory conditions)
  • Make informed decisions about exercise and commuting
  • Understand environmental conditions in your area

Data Sources

We source our air quality data from Open-Meteo, a trusted provider of meteorological and environmental data. All measurements follow international air quality standards.

Our Commitment

We're committed to:

  • Providing accurate, timely air quality information
  • Maintaining a fast, reliable platform
  • Protecting user privacy (we don't collect personal data)
  • Continuous improvement based on user feedback
  • Making air quality information accessible to everyone
